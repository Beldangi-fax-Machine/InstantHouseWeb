Our Story

A fusion of cultures, a passion for quality.

Bridging Cultures Through Taste

Instant House is more than just a convenience store; it is a culinary destination in the heart of Essex Junction. Founded with a vision to bring the rich, authentic flavors of Korea and India to Vermont, we have curated a selection of products that tell a story of heritage and tradition.

We believe that great food connects people. Whether you are an experienced chef looking for a specific spice or a curious foodie wanting to try authentic kimchi for the first time, our shelves are stocked with you in mind. We prioritize quality, authenticity, and freshness in every item we offer.
